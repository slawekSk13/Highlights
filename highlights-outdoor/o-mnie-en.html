A lot of what I currently do, I started rather late and sort of coincidentally. I picked up skiing at 16 during a trip, where I was supposed to trek. 2 years later, on a trip to Crimea, my photography adventure has started. Another 2 years have passed and, again by chance, I began climbing.

All things considered, I don’t believe in coincidence. What I do believe in is that you’re given na opportunity and it’s up to you to take it. I consistently developed both my passions: mountains and art during university and later at a photography school, whilst also working at a mountain store. Having 10 years of experience in photography and videography, working on events and being advanced in studio and portrait photography, I now strive to implement my experience where I feel most at home: the woods and mountains.

I find joy in working with enthusiasts, be it tour operators, gear manufacturers or athletes who work on improving the equipment by putting it to the test.

They inspire me not only to continuously perfect my photography and videography skills, but also to advance in other sport disciplines. Alpine skiing, freeride, various types of cycling, rock climbing, trekking, running - this all gives me evergy to face day-to-day challenges.

To paraphrase - enough talk, let the pictures speak for themselves. If you need photos or a film of your products, services or adventures and my work speaks to you, don’t hesitate to contact me for more information.
